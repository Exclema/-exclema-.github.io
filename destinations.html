Popular Destinations

  • Masai Mara National Reserve: Witness the Great Migration and big cats.
  • Diani Beach: Enjoy white sandy beaches and water sports.
  • Mount Kenya: Climb Africa's second-highest peak.
  • Lake Naivasha: Spot flamingos and hippos.
  • Nairobi National Park: See wildlife with a city skyline backdrop.
